Are you an Elmscribe?

Elmscribes are curious. Elmscribes dare to cross boundaries in the search for wisdom.  They seek the link among experiences, are enthused in the company of virtue and talent, revel in the generation of ideas, and influence change, for a better, wiser, world.

Founded June 2015, Elmscribes Honour Society stands to encourage the scholarly and entrepreneurial pursuits of its scribehood. We are now in the connections and collections phase as we reach out to connect members and establish a literary base. Elmscribes will grow to be a tank that thinks and an idea that works such that our collective minds can meld to serve our immediate societies and the world.
